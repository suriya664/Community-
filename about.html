About Activity Hub

Your premier platform for activity registration and membership management. Discover exciting activities and connect with our vibrant community.

Our Story

Our Mission

To create a warm, welcoming space where creativity flourishes and genuine connections are made. We believe in the power of community, art, and shared experiences.

Our Vision

To be London's most inspiring creative community space, where every individual feels valued, supported, and empowered to express their authentic self.

Our Core Values

Inclusivity

We welcome everyone, regardless of background or skill level.

Excellence

We strive for the highest quality in every event and activity.

Growth

Continuous learning and personal development are at our core.

Our Journey

2020 - Foundation

Community Form was born from a vision to create a warm, creative community space where connections flourish.

2021 - First Events

We hosted our first art workshops and community gatherings, building connections one event at a time.

2022 - Growing Community

Our community grew to over 200 members, with regular workshops, exhibitions, and social gatherings.

2023 - Expansion

We expanded our space and programs, offering more creative opportunities and community events.

2024 - Today

Continuing to grow and evolve, creating meaningful connections and inspiring creativity every day.
